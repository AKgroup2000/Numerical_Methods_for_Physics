Numerical Methods

Code and homework for the graduate class taught by Prof. Mike Zingale at Stony Brook University, 2013: Integration and Differentiation: numerical derivatives, error estimates, Simpson's rule, Ordinary Differential Equations: Eulder and Euler-Cromer methods, Verlet method, Linear Algebra and FFTs: time series for the damped driven pendulum, matrix inverse, conditional number, Advection and Hyperbolic Partial Differential Equations: implicit advection, Lax Wendroff, non-linear hyperbolic equations, Elliptic Partial Differential Equations, Computational Fluid Dynamics: Rayleigh-Taylor Instabilities.
